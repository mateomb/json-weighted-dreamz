What are Weighted Blankets

Weighted Dreamz is our up and coming company that aims to provide our users with weighted blankets at an affordable price regardless of their location!

What are weighted blankets? Weighted blankets have been around for many years. They are used to treat anxiety and after trauma, where they are known as comfort blankets. They are also recommended by therapists and psychologists to help with both psychological and physical problems such as anxiety and insomnia. The blankets are weighted with internal ball weights, it is recommended that the user takes a blanket with ~10% of his body weight.
